The best movies I've watched in the last couple of years

Top 3

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump is a classic American film that tells the story of a simple man named Forrest who experiences some of the most significant events in American history. The movie is a heartwarming and inspiring tale of a man who overcomes adversity and lives a fulfilling life. Tom Hanks delivers an outstanding performance as Forrest, and the supporting cast is equally impressive. The film is a must-watch for anyone who appreciates a well-crafted story and exceptional acting. Overall, Forrest Gump is a timeless masterpiece that will continue to captivate audiences for generations to come.

Whitney Houston. Dance with me.

Whitney Houston's biopic "I Will Always Love You" is a touching tribute to the legendary singer's life and legacy. The movie captures Houston's rise to fame, her struggles with addiction and relationships, and her unparalleled talent. The lead actress delivers a powerful performance that does justice to Houston's iconic voice and personality. Overall, "I Will Always Love You" is a must-watch for fans of Whitney Houston and anyone looking for an inspiring story about perseverance and triumph over adversity.
